About Me

My name is Soodabeh, a passionate artist and painting graduate dedicated to both creating and teaching art. For years, I have been immersed in the world of visual arts — developing my own artistic practice while sharing my knowledge with others.

Art, for me, is more than a profession; it is a lifelong passion. I approach every class and every artwork with care, creativity, and a deep love for the process.

Teaching

I believe that art is for everyone. I work with students of all ages — from as young as 4 to as experienced as 90. My classes combine practical skills with an understanding of art history, helping students connect technique with meaning.

Techniques & Mediums

I teach a wide range of artistic techniques including acrylic painting, watercolor, oil and soft pastels, ecoline, colored pencils, and mixed media. I also specialize in sculpting and creative work with clay.

My Work

Alongside teaching, I actively create my own artworks — paintings and unique, handcrafted clay pieces with an expressive approach. I enjoy experimenting with different materials to create meaningful and visually engaging pieces.

Mediums Acrylic, Watercolor, Pastels, Clay
Students Ages 4 to 90
Based in Vancouver, BC
